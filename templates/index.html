Impact

CURRRENT SITUATION

Federal Level

  • Bill C-237 also known as An Act to Establish a Framework on Diabetes in Canada
  • Development of Diabetes 360 framework
  • "Healthy Eating Strategy" launched by Health Canada
  • Expanded public funding for intermittently-scanned Continuous Glucose Monitoring system to include Freestyle Libre 2

Provincial level

  • OHIP+ provides over 4,400 drug products at no cost for Ontarians <24 years of age who are not covered by private plan
  • Ontario Drug Benefit and Trillium Drug Program (Seniors >65 years with high prescription drug costs relative to their income)
  • The Monitoring for Health Program
  • Insulin Syringes for Seniors Program provides $170/year
  • The Diabetes Complications Prevention Strategy (DCPS)
  • Ontario's Diabetes Prevention Program
  • The Pediatric Diabetes Initiative
  • The Ontario Aboriginal Diabetes Strategy
  • Assistive Devices Program (ADP)

Municipal level

  • Northern Diabetes Health Network
  • Southern Ontario Aboriginal Diabetes Initiative (SOADI)
  • Seniors and Diabetes Initiative
  • Diabetes Complication Prevention Strategy
  • Evaluation- Ontario Diabetes Status Index

Emmanuel Ofori

I am an International Medical Graduate from Ghana with interests in Internal and Family Medicine. I also have a Diploma in Tropical Medicine and Hygiene from the Royal College of Physicians, United Kingdom. I am the Official Doctor for the “Bisa Health App” a health application where individuals can reach doctors for health advice on their phones and targeted towards the health needs of the less privileged and those in the rural areas. I have also assisted in a number of research projects including the Stroke Investigative Research and Education Network (SIREN), Randomised Evaluation of COVID-19 Therapy (RECOVERY TRIAL), Systematic Investigation of Blacks with Stroke - Genomics (SIBS-Genomics) and Barekuma Community Collaborative Development Project (BCCDP). I have massive love for Public health and preventive medicine and therefore I have embarked on Health screening exercises on numerous occasions with different organizations in various parts of Ghana. I have co-authored over ten (11) peer-reviewed articles( https://www.researchgate.net/profile/Emmanuel-Ofori-24/research) published in esteemed journals which span a range of topics including COVID-19, stroke, Systemic lupus erythematosus, HIV, hypertension, diabetes mellitus and kidney diseases. I enjoy Health / Business (Investment) related seminars and am a member of several associations and groups including Reachout World Mission, Apt foundation for sexual health, Immigrant Researchers Support Network (IRSN), Alberta International Medical Graduates Association (AIMGA) and Tropical Disease Research (TDR) Global( World Health Organization). I am also a web developer and data analyst.
